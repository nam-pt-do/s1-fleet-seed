Glossary

    A
  • Asset
    Anything of value in your process that can range from raw materials and feedstock, to piping systems, to rotating and reciprocating machinery, to storage tanks, to your final product.
    • D
  • DAQ
    Data acquisition (system)
    • F
  • Fleet
    Multiple business sites or enterprises.
    • M
  • Machine
    Rotating and reciprocating machinery, such as turbines and compressors.
    • O
  • Operating State
    This describes the machine running conditions. These operating states can be Running, Not Running, or Unknown.
    • P
  • Point
    A location where data is collected. A point can represent a physical location, such as a measurement transducer or a specific output from a software application (or control system) that calculates a value and sends the result to the System 1 DAQ.
    • S
  • Site
    A place where trains or assets are located.
    • T
  • Train
    A group of machines that share a common driver or driven machine, a common shaft, or several shafts connected through a gearbox.
    • V
  • Variable
    A particular type of information that can be obtained from data collected at a point. Several variables can be associated to a point.
