System 1 Fleet Management Introduction

GE's System 1 Fleet Management is a Web-based software application (portal) for monitoring and diagnosing the overall health of your Fleet. A Fleet is comprised of Sites, Folders, Trains, Machine-Trains and Machines. A mobile version of the application is available for use on iPad and iPhones.
