Using the Links in the Name Column to Navigate the Fleet Hierarchy

  1. In the Name column, click the name of the Site or other Fleet component.
  2. An Open Folder pop-up is displayed.
  3. The Grid View is updated with the contents of the folder.
  4. Continue to click the Name in the Name column until you have reached the site, folder, train, train-machine or machine you want to view.

Selecting a Customer and a Fleet

To select a customer and a Fleet, complete the following procedure.

  1. Click the down arrow in the Customer List box . The customer names displayed in the list box are defined by the System 1 administrator when the System 1 Fleet Management account creates your account.
  2. Click the down arrow in the Select drop-down list . A list of sites associated with the selected customer is displayed.
  3. Select a site. The names of folders, machine-trains, and machines within the selected site are displayed.
  4. Click the right-facing arrows to expand the contents of the folders, machine-trains, or machines.
