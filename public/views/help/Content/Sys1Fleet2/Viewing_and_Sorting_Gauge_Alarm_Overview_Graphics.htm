Working With the Gauges

The Gauges view on the Overview page shows the health of the site, folder, train, machine and more.

Launching the Gauge View

  1. From the Dashboard, select a Fleet and Hierarchy element. The Grid View is displayed.
  2. Click the Gauge icon .
  3. The page displays system health data from a set of gauges. The first of the following two figures shows the output from a typical of a set of gauges. The second of the following two figures shows an example of a typical 30-day alarm trend graphic. To generate the second view, hover the mouse over an index on the gauge for a few seconds and click on the + that appears.


Viewing Data Using the Gauges

The gauge graphics contain alarm threshold indicators and needle indicators, and a method to drill-down in the Fleet hierarchy (see the following figure and table).

Sorting the Data Displayed in the Gauges

In the Sort by menu, select:

  • Name
  • Asset Protection
  • Asset Management
  • Asset Combined
  • Instrumentation Protection
  • Instrumentation Management
  • Instrumentation Combined
  • SmartSignal
  • Performance - This parameter is displayed for System 1 Fleet Management Trains and Machines that are installed with the Bently Thermal Performance package. in

Viewing a 30-day Alarm Trend in a Gauge Format

The four steps in this section explain how to launch a graph that displays the quantity and severity of alarms during the past 30 days.

  1. Near the upper-right corner of the gauge, click and hold the mouse over the index you are interested in trending
  2. Click the icon. The gauge display expands to display the alarm trends for the past 30 days. A line chart labeled 30 Day Trend is displayed below the selected gauge.
  3. To close the 30 Day Trend view, click the icon.
