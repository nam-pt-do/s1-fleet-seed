About Alarm Severity Designations

This section describes the legacy method of how System 1 and SmartSignal designated alarm severity, and the new method used by System 1 Fleet Management to designate System 1 and SmartSignal alarm severity.

Legacy Alarm Severity Designation Methods

System 1 used an alarm severity designation method of 1-4, where 1 was the least severe alarm, and 4 was the most severe alarm.

SmartSignal used an alarm severity designation method of 1-n, where 1 was the most severe alarm, and n was the least severe alarm.

System 1 Fleet Management Alarm Severity Designation Methods

In System 1 Fleet Management, the System 1 and SmartSignal alarm severity designations were combined. The outcome is a method that designates alarm severity in three categories: High (shown in red), Medium (shown in yellow) and Low (orange) to display the alarms (see the following Default normalization table).

 

The Dashboard provides administrator-level to map the System 1 event severity levels and SmartSignal priority levels to the common scale at the Fleet level (see the following figure).
