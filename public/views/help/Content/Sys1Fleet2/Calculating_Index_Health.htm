Computing Index Health Trends

Fleet Index health trends s computed by averaging the alarms, their frequency, and their severity over a period of time.

Example 1: On October 1, 2014:

  • Thirty-three percent of generated alarms generated were of low severity
  • Sixty-six percent of generated alarms were of medium severity

Example 2: On October 8, 2014:

  • Fifty percent of generated alarms generated were of medium severity
  • Twenty percent of generated alarms were of low severity
  • Thirty percent of generated alarms were of high severity

Example 2: On October 15, 2014:

  • Fifty percent of generated alarms generated were of low severity
  • Ten percent of generated alarms were of high severity
  • Forty percent of generated alarms were of medium severity

The health index calculated for those three weeks in October would be an average of the total low, medium and high alarms generated against the total alarms available for the three weeks in October.
