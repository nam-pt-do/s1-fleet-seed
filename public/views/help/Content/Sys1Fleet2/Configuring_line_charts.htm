Generating Line Charts

Use the Line Chart generation feature on the Alarms tab to create line charts that show alarm trends for different enterprises in a selected fleet and hierarchy. You can hold the mouse over a point in the chart for a few seconds and view the number of alarm(s) that occurred on the date the alarm occurred.

To generate a line chart, complete the following steps;

  1. From the Dashboard page, click Alarms. The Alarms page is displayed.
  2. In the View by: drop-down, select Subgroup or Event Type.
  3. In the Alarm List Range: drop-down, click Active, 1 Month, 6 Months, and 1 Year.
  4. Click the line chart icon . The chart is generated and displayed in the top section of the Alarms page..
