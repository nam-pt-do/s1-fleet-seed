Editing General Preferences

In this section of the Preferences page, you can modify Time Zone, Auto-Refresh, and Primary Hierarchy settings. The following figure shows the General preference settings.

 

To edit General Preferences, complete the following steps;

To do This Follow These Steps
Change the Time Zone Click GMT or Local Time.
Change the Auto-Refresh settings Click Auto-Refresh. Set the Refresh interval from the default setting of five minutes. The minimum setting is three minutes.
Change the systems listed in the Primary Hierarchy Click System 1 or SmartSignal. Primary means that the selected hierarchy will be shown first in the hierarchy drop-down list.
