Viewing and Editing Your Preference Settings

Editing Preferences

You have the option to modify a subset of default Preferences settings on the General Settings, Overview Page, and Alarms Page.

  1. From the home page, click the down arrow next to your user profile name .
  2. Click Preferences. The Preferences page is displayed.
  3. Change settings as required.
  4. Complete one of the following steps:
  • Click Save Changes to save your changes.
  • Click Cancel to close the Preferences page without saving any changes.
  • Click Restore Default Settings to reset the previous Preferences settings.
