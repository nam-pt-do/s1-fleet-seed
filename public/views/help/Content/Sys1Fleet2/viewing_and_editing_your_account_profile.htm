Editing Your Account Profile

The Account Profile pane displays your Username, Role, Name, and Email address. You can change your Name and email address. Depending on system configuration, the Account Profile page might include the option to change the password for your account.

To update your account profile, complete these steps;

  1. From the Dashboard module, click the down arrow next to your user name.
  2. Click Account Profile. The Account Profile page is displayed.
  3. Complete one or more of the steps in the table below to edit your Account Profile settings.
  4. After you complete the changes, click Save. Changes to your Account Profile take effect immediately.
To do this Follow these steps
Change your name in the database In the Name box, enter a new name.
Change your Email address In the Email box, enter a new email address.

Option: Change password
  1. Click the Change Password link. Three new boxes are displayed.

  2. In the Current Password box, enter the current password.
  3. In the New Password box, enter the new password.
  4. In the Confirm Password box, re-enter the new password.
  5. Click Save to change the password changes. Click Don't Change Password to cancel the password changes.
