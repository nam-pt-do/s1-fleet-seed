Index Table Health Indicators

To generate an index table, drill down in the hierarchy and select a Hierarchy, such as a System 1 Hierarchy site, folder, train, or train-machine.

Column Title Description
Name The items, such as a machine or train-machine that are within the selected site, folder or site.
Status

If you select a site or drill down two folder levels within a site, the column indicates the communication status of the . A green bar is displayed if communication is occurring.

If you drill down to a third-level folder, the column indicates the running

AP The System 1 Asset Protection alarm percentage. Out of the percentage of all alarms available, this icon gives the percentage of alarms that are being used.
AM The System 1 Asset Management alarm percentage. Out of the percentage of all alarms available, this icon gives the percentage of alarms that are being used.
IP The System 1 Instrumentation Protection alarm percentage
IM The System 1 Instrumentation Management alarm percentage
SS  
Total Alarms This column displays the total alarms in the selected folder, machine-train or machine. The number(s) in the column are links. Click the link to open the Alarms page and view a bar chart of the total alarms.
High The number of High-Severity alarms in the folder, train-machine or machine.The number(s) in the column are links. Click the link to open the Alarms page and view a bar chart of the total alarm count and a breakdown bythe site, machine-train, or machine.
Medium The number of Medium-Severity alarms in the folder, train-machine or machine.The number(s) in the column are links. Click the link to open the Alarms page and view a bar chart of the total medium-severity alarms.
Low The number of Low-Severity alarms in the folder, train-machine or machine.The number(s) in the column are links. Click the link to open the Alarms page and view a bar chart of the total low-severity alarms.
