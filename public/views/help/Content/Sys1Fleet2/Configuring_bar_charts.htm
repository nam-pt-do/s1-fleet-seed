Generating bar Charts

The bar chart shows the quantity of alarms and their associated severity for a selected item, which can be Enterprise, Folder, Train, or Machine. The bars in the chart are organized by the normalized severity colors.

To generate a bar chart, complete the following steps;

  1. From the Dashboard page, click Alarms. The Alarms page is displayed for your current selection, that is, customer and any item selected from the hierarchy.
  2. Select a Fleet and an item from Hierarchies menu (see the following illustration for an example).


    After you select a customer and an item, a default bar chart is displayed. The top half of the page shows a bar chart.
  3. In the View By: drop-down, click Subgroup or Event Type. Event Types for System 1, including Asset Management, Asset Protection, Instrument Management and Instrument Protection. An Event type forSmartSignal iis Asset Management.
  4. In the Alarm List Range: drop-down menu, click Active, 1 Month, 6 Months, and 1 Year.
  5. The chart is generated and displayed in the top section of the Alarms page.
  6. Option: Click and hold the mouse for a few seconds over a section of the chart to view a summary of the alarms in the selected section of the chart..
