Navigating to an Asset Level

When you select an asset, the view contains the collective machine data for that asset.

  1. If applicable, select a fleet on the left, then click the drop-down arrow on the right to open the asset hierarchy.

  2. Click a icon to display that asset level. Continue clicking the icons until you reach the level to view.

  3. Click the asset to display the Overview page. Your view (grid or gauge) appears according to your application preferences.

    From there, you can click tabs to navigate to the Alarms or Analysis pages.
