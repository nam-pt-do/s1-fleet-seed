Application Administration

The System 1 Fleet Managementapplication superuser creates at least one application administrator role when completing the installation and configuration. The application administrator is responsible for:

  • Creating users.
  • Editing users.
  • Deleting users.
  • Editing enterprise data.
  • Activating or deactivating enterprises.
  • Deleting enterprises.
