Modifying a Plot or Preset

To edit a plot or preset:

  1. Complete the steps in Creating and Saving a New Analysis Plot or Running a Plot Using a Preset.

  2. When the chart appears, click the icon on the far right to open the Edit Chart screen.

  3. Select an asset from the hierarchy. When you open the hierarchy, the list extends to the selected asset, but you need to click the asset again to confirm.
  4. Select one or more different points or tags to add to the chart.

To delete an existing point or tag, click the Current Selections hyperlink, then click the Delete button in the applicable row.

You can also use the following options:

  1. When your selections are complete, click Update Chart in the bottom-right corner to view the updated chart. Your revised selections are identified in the legend on the right.
  2. If you are editing a preset, click the Save button to save the preset with the updated settings.
  3. To refresh the data at any time, click the icon.
