Asset Hierarchy

The Dashboard page appears when you log into the application, and displays your default view.

Your fleet names (if more than one) are shown in the drop-down list on the left, and the asset hierarchy corresponding to the selected fleet is represented in the drop-down list on the right.

Your asset hierarchy is organized by the following asset classifications:

Icon Level Description

Enterprise

Organizational location (site).

Folder

Category layer, where assets are organized into separations that make sense in the structure, such as process or utility systems.

Train

A group of machines that function interactively.

Machine

An individual piece of equipment, such as a motor, gearbox, compressor, pump, and so forth.
 

NOTE: In this document, each hierarchy level is called an "asset," but the data is always derived from the associated machines and consolidated for the selected level.

When you select an asset at any level, the assets at the child level appear in the view. For example, if you select an individual folder in the asset hierarchy, the trains in the folder are displayed, as shown below.

The path to your selected asset in the hierarchy is displayed in breadcrumbs above the table.
