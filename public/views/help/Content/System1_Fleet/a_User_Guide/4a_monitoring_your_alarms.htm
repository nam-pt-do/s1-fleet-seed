Monitoring Your Alarms

  1. Click the Alarms tab or from the Grid View table or click the number hyperlink in the Total Alarms column. The Alarms page opens, displaying the alarms plots and alarms table containing the active alarms on the selected asset.

  2. To change the column array, click the icon to include more or fewer columns.

    The new display overrides the default for the session, but is not persistent for future sessions.

  3. To filter for certain alarms only, click the icon to add the filter row to the table, then select or enter your filter criteria for each applicable column. The content filters accordingly.

  4. To hide the filter row, click the icon again. Hiding the filter fields does not clear any filters you have specified.
  5. To clear the filters, click the icon.
