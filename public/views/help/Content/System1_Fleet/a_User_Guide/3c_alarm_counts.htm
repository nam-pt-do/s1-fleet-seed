Alarm Counts

Active alarm counts are displayed in the Gauge and Grid views:

You can click the Total Alarms number hyperlink to navigate to the Alarms page for the asset.

Active alarm counts are considered in the health index algorithm calculations. If a large number of alarms have been triggered on an asset, the asset health may be in the Alert or Danger zone, even though the highest level active alarm is at a lower severity. For information on your health indexes and zones, and how they are calculated, see Asset Health Indexes.
